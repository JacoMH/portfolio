Feature Breakdown
Landlords

The Landlord has the ability to add the information for a building, detailing the name, building address, year the buidling was made and the type of building they are dealing with.

The Landlord can also calculate the buildings EER rating by clicking the calculate button next to the record. The user enters numerical information into a form which then calculates the rating and the band the building falls within. The landlord can also view their calculation history by going to the designated tab.

The building information can be viewed by clicking the "view" button. It displays a diagram of the EER rating and the band, but also displays the building information and the EER calculation history. However, if the Landlord wants to edit the building information, they click the "edit" button and change the information accordingly.

The calculation history of the buildings and the Landlords profile information, which can be changed, is shown here.

Tenants

Tenants create an account, and are then prompted to enter the building address they are staying in. If the address matches a building in the database, it is displayed on their dashboard and they can view the information on the EER rating, building details etc. However they cannot change anything.

Admins

Admin has the ability to delete records of EER calculations of any user.

Change this

The admin has the ability to view user information, update user information or delete their account entirely.
